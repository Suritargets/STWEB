## The Reality

Financial fraud costs Caribbean organisations billions every year. Yet forensic accounting remains underutilised in the region.

## What Is Forensic Accounting?

Forensic accounting combines accounting expertise with investigative skills to detect, analyse and document financial irregularities.

## When Do You Need It?

- Suspicion of fraud or irregularities
- Due diligence in acquisitions
- Dispute resolution
- Compliance investigations
