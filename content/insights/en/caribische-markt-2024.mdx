## Introduction

The Caribbean is undergoing a rapid digital transformation. Suriname faces unique opportunities, but also specific challenges that are not visible in international reports.

## Key Trends

### 1. Mobile-first Economy

More than 78% of internet users in Suriname access the internet exclusively via mobile. This requires a fundamentally different approach from what European or North American models prescribe.

### 2. Local Payment Systems

International payment gateways do not always work reliably in the region. Businesses that succeed integrate local alternatives.

### 3. Trust as Currency

In small communities, reputation is everything. Digital platforms that combine personal contact with online convenience perform significantly better.

## Conclusion

The opportunities are there — but they require local expertise and cultural understanding. That is exactly what Suritargets provides.
