## Background

A financial institution wanted to introduce new savings products but lacked sufficient insight into the needs and behaviour of the Surinamese consumer.

## Methodology

We conducted a mixed-methods study:
- **Quantitative**: 400 surveys distributed across Paramaribo and districts
- **Qualitative**: 20 in-depth interviews with target group representatives
- **Desk research**: analysis of existing market data and competitive analysis

## Findings

The research revealed three critical insights that directly influenced product development.

## Impact

The recommendations were fully implemented in the new product portfolio.
