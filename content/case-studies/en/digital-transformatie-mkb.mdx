## The Challenge

An established retail business in Paramaribo wanted to digitise their operations. They had no online presence, their inventory was tracked in Excel files, and customers could not order online.

## Our Approach

We started with a thorough analysis of their current processes and identified four critical areas for improvement:

1. **Inventory management** — real-time inventory tracking
2. **Online store** — product display and order processing
3. **POS integration** — linking online and physical sales
4. **Reporting** — weekly revenue overviews

## The Solution

We built a fully integrated solution based on Next.js and a cloud database. The system now processes an average of 150 orders per week.

## Results

- **40%** revenue increase within 3 months of launch
- **150+** online orders per week
- **Real-time** inventory management across 3 locations
- **2 hours** per day saved on administration
